## Dream Island

**Description:** Special islands you can visit once per day to get rare items and ore such as gold and Pokemetal. Unlocked after befriending Drifloon (Dream Island specialty). Islands have no wild Pokemon but contain items, recipes, and resources. The island resets at the end of the day — anything left behind is lost.

**Objective:** Strip the island of resources and return before the day ends.

### Legendary Encounters

When visiting, there is a chance to find: Raikou, Entei, Suicune, Volcanion, or Mewtwo.

### Rare Finds

Each trip can yield:
- Notebooks with hidden lore
- Outfits based on main series characters (Blue, Green, Ethan, etc.)
- Rare items including Pokemetal Ingots
- Tips for undiscovered habitats
